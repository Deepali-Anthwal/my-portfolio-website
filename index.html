Favourite Hobby: Arts and Crafts

I find immense joy in the process of creation—taking raw materials and transforming them into something tangible. Whether it's paper crafting or DIY projects, this hobby allows me to experiment with textures, colors, and structures.

Benefits of Arts and Crafts:

  1. Patience and Focus: Crafting takes time and a steady hand. It has taught me how to stay focused until the very last detail is finished.
  2. Hands-on Creativity: It’s a great break from the screen. Working with my hands helps me clear my head and think more clearly.
  3. The Joy of Building: I love the feeling of finishing a project from scratch, whether it's a paper model or a piece of software.
